Let's create something great together

If you’re looking for a smaller company that provides direct, one-on-one support, look no further. Unity is a no-BS contractor — we tell it like it is. No corporate runaround, no price gouging. Just realistic pricing, a skilled team, and a simple, honest process. We’re here to work.

Unity Construction and Design is a full-service, licensed general contractor based in La Mesa and serving all of San Diego County. We specialize in custom home remodeling, kitchen and bathroom renovations, concrete driveways, patios, ADU (Accessory Dwelling Unit) construction, garage conversions, deck and fence building, and complete home improvement projects. As a trusted San Diego contractor, we provide affordable construction services with a focus on quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction. Whether you’re looking for a La Mesa general contractor for residential remodeling, a San Diego home improvement company for outdoor living spaces, or an experienced builder for new additions and renovations, Unity Construction and Design delivers dependable results. Our team of local builders have years of experience in home remodeling, design-build services, structural upgrades, and property improvements across La Mesa, San Diego, and surrounding East County communities. From modern kitchen remodels and bathroom upgrades to durable concrete installations and custom deck projects, we’re committed to creating lasting value in every project. If you’re searching for a reliable San Diego general contractor who specializes in remodeling, renovations, and custom design, Unity Construction and Design is here to help bring your vision to life.
